Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 1, 2025

Tabang ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use the Tabang app and website. Please read this policy carefully.

1. Who We Are

Tabang is a community disaster response and mutual aid platform built for the people of Aklan, Philippines. Our mission is to connect residents, volunteers, and local emergency responders during disasters.

Contact: support@tabang.app
Location: Aklan, Philippines

2. Information We Collect

We may collect the following information when you register and use Tabang:

  • Account Information: Name, email address, and password.
  • Profile Information: Barangay or municipality, contact number (optional).
  • Location Data: General location when you report an emergency or request assistance, with your permission.
  • Usage Data: Pages visited, features used, and timestamps — to improve the app.
  • User-Generated Content: Emergency reports, messages, or assistance requests you submit.

We do not collect payment information. Tabang is free to use.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage your account.
  • To connect you with community members, volunteers, and responders during emergencies.
  • To display emergency reports and assistance requests on the community map.
  • To send you relevant alerts and notifications (with your consent).
  • To improve and maintain the Tabang platform.
  • To comply with legal obligations.

4. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal data. We may share information in the following limited cases:

  • With other users: Emergency reports and assistance requests you post may be visible to other Tabang users in your area.
  • With service providers: We may use third-party services (e.g., hosting, analytics) that process data on our behalf under strict confidentiality agreements.
  • With authorities: If required by law or to protect public safety during a declared emergency.

5. Data Retention

We retain your account data for as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your account and data at any time by emailing support@tabang.app. Emergency report data may be retained for community safety records.

6. Data Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational security measures to protect your data. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We encourage you to use a strong password and protect your account credentials.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request correction of inaccurate data.
  • Request deletion of your account and data.
  • Withdraw consent for location access at any time (via your device settings).

To exercise any of these rights, email us at support@tabang.app.

8. Children's Privacy

Tabang is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe a child has provided us with their information, please contact us immediately.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify registered users of significant changes via email or in-app notification. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page reflects the most recent revision.

📬 Contact Us

If you have questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy or your data, please reach out:
